## Visual Guidelines

### Image Sizes
- **Twitter/X:** 1200x675px (16:9)
- **LinkedIn:** 1200x627px
- **Instagram:** 1080x1080px (square) or 1080x1350px (portrait)
- **GitHub:** 1200x630px (for social preview)

### Color Usage
- Use brand colors (#12f6ff, #ff906f, #14c9c9)
- Dark backgrounds (#11172b, #0b1224)
- High contrast for readability

### Typography
- Headlines: Comfortaa, bold
- Body: Comfortaa, regular
- Keep text minimal on graphics

## Content Themes

### Project Launches
- Focus on impact and outcomes
- Include tech stack
- Call for collaboration
- Use oceanic metaphors sparingly

### Technical Insights
- Share learnings and solutions
- Include code snippets when relevant
- Explain complex concepts clearly
- Engage with community questions

### Community Engagement
- Respond to comments authentically
- Share others' work
- Participate in discussions
- Build relationships

## Posting Schedule Suggestions

- **Twitter/X:** 2-3 times per day
- **LinkedIn:** 2-3 times per week
- **GitHub:** As projects are completed
- **Discord:** Regular community engagement

## Engagement Tips

1. **Respond Promptly:** Engage with comments and messages
2. **Be Authentic:** Share real experiences and learnings
3. **Add Value:** Provide insights, not just updates
4. **Build Community:** Support others' work
5. **Stay Consistent:** Regular posting builds presence

---

*Remember: Quality over quantity. Each post should add value to your community.*
