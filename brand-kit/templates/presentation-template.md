## Slide Design Guidelines

### Color Scheme
- **Background:** #11172b (Deep Ocean) or #0b1224 (Midnight Depths)
- **Primary Text:** #f4f8ff (Light Surface)
- **Accents:** #12f6ff (Cyan), #ff906f (Coral)
- **Secondary Text:** #c5d8ff (Sky Reflection)

### Typography
- **Titles:** Comfortaa, Bold (700), 36-48px
- **Headings:** Comfortaa, SemiBold (600), 24-32px
- **Body:** Comfortaa, Regular (400), 18-20px
- **Accent Text:** Comfortaa, Medium (500), 14-16px, Uppercase

## Design Elements

### Backgrounds
- Solid dark colors (#11172b, #0b1224)
- Subtle gradients (radial from corners)
- Oceanic patterns (waves, depth effects)

### Accents
- Glow effects on key elements
- Gradient overlays for depth
- Wave dividers between sections

### Icons
- Use brand icon set
- Size: 48-64px for emphasis
- Color: #12f6ff or #ff906f

## Content Guidelines

### Slide Content
- **One idea per slide**
- **Maximum 5-7 bullet points**
- **Large, readable text**
- **High contrast for visibility**

### Visual Hierarchy
1. Title (largest, most prominent)
2. Key points (medium, clear)
3. Supporting details (smaller, secondary color)
4. Accents (color highlights)

### Transitions
- Smooth, subtle animations
- Fade or slide transitions
- Avoid distracting effects
- Maintain professional tone

## Presentation Structure

### Opening (3-5 slides)
1. Title slide
2. About / Introduction
3. Agenda / Overview

### Main Content (10-15 slides)
4. Section 1: [Topic]
5. Section 2: [Topic]
6. Section 3: [Topic]
7. Examples / Case Studies
8. Key Takeaways

### Closing (2-3 slides)
9. Summary
10. Next Steps
11. Contact / Q&A

1. **Consistency:** Use brand colors and fonts throughout
2. **Clarity:** Keep slides simple and focused
3. **Visual Appeal:** Use images and graphics strategically
4. **Accessibility:** Ensure high contrast and readable text
5. **Brand Alignment:** Maintain oceanic theme subtly
